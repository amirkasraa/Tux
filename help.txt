`?help` For help message.
`?quote` For random quote from zenquotes.io.
`?nonsense` For random nonsense from database.
`?newns` For add new nonsense to database.
example : `?newns YOUR NONSENSE`
`?delns` For delete nonsense from database.
use index for delete.
example : `?delns INDEX`
`?listns` For nonsense list.
`?conj` For conjugation verb in french, Made by mlconjug3.
tenses that conjugation verb in are => Présent, Imparfait, Futur, Passé Simple
example : `?conj VERBE`
